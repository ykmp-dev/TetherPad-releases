TetherPad

FTP tether capture for iPad. Your camera sends photos straight to your iPad over Wi-Fi.

Install via AltStore

Add this source to your AltStore:

https://ykmp-dev.github.io/TetherPad-releases/source.json Open in AltStore

Requirements

  • iPad running iOS 16 or later
  • Apple ID (free tier OK — 7-day refresh via AltServer)
  • Wi-Fi network shared with your camera
  • AltStore installed on iPad (altstore.io)

TetherPad is distributed via AltStore. Each user signs with their own Apple ID.
